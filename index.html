一、

1. 求下列各函数的全微分：

(1) $z=x^{3}+y^{3}-3xy$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=(3x^{2}-3y)dx+(3y^{2}-3x)dy \end{align*}$$

(2) $z=x^{2}y^{3}$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=2xy^{3}dx+3x^{2}y^{2}dy \end{align*}$$

(3) $z=\frac{x^2-y^{2}}{x^{2}+y^{2}}$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=\frac{4xy^{2}}{(x^{2}+y^{2})^{2}}dx-\frac{4x^{2}y}{(x^{2}+y^{2})^{2}}dy \end{align*}$$
注意：$z=\frac{x^{2}-y^{2}}{x^{2}+y^{2}}=1-2\frac{y^{2}}{x^{2}+y^2}=-1+2\frac{x^{2}}{x^{2}+y^{2}}$

(4) $z=sin^{2}x+cos^{2}y$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=2sinxcosxdx+2cosy(-siny)dy\\ &=sin2x dx - sin2y dy \end{align*}$$

(5) $z=yx^{y}$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=yyx^{y-1}dx+(x^{y}+yx^{y}lnx)dy\\ &=y^{2}x^{y-1}dx+(1+ylnx)x^{y}dy \end{align*}$$
注意：对$x$还是对$y$求偏导数

(6) $z=ln(x^{2}+y^{2})$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=\frac{2x}{x^{2}+y^{2}}dx+\frac{2y}{x^{2}+y^{2}}dy \end{align*}$$

2. 求函数$z=\frac{y}{x}$在$x=2$,$y=1$,$\Delta x=0.1$,$\Delta y=-0.2$时的全增量$\Delta z$和全微分$dz$

$$\begin{align*} \Delta z &= f(x+\Delta x,y+\Delta y)-f(x,y)\\ &= f(2.1,0.8)-f(2,1)\\ &= \frac{0.8}{2.1}-\frac{1}{2}\\ &= -\frac{5}{42}\\ dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=-\frac{y}{x^{2}}dx+\frac{1}{x}dy \end{align*}$$

故$dz|_{(2,1)}=-\frac{1}{4}dx+\frac{1}{2}dy=-\frac{1}{4}\cdot 0.1+\frac{1}{2}\cdot (-0.2)=-0.125$

3. 求函数$z=ln(2+x^{2}+y^{2})$在$x=2$,$y=1$时的全微分

$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=\frac{2x}{2+x^{2}+y^{2}}dx+\frac{2y}{2+x^{2}+y^{2}}dy \end{align*}$$

故$dz|_{(2,1)}=\frac{4}{7}dx+\frac{2}{7}dy$

4. 求下列各函数的全微分：

(1) $u=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}$
$$\begin{align*} du &= \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}dy + \frac{\partial u}{\partial z}dz\\ &=\frac{x}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}}dx+\frac{y}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}}dy+\frac{z}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}}dz \end{align*}$$

(2) $u=e^{x+yz}$
$$\begin{align*} du &= \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}dy + \frac{\partial u}{\partial z}dz\\ &=e^{x+yz}dx+ze^{x+yz}dy+ye^{x+yz}dz \end{align*}$$

二、

1. $z=arctan\frac{x}{1+y^{2}}$, $dz|_{(1,1)}$

$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=\frac{1}{1+(\frac{x}{1+y^{2}})^{2}}\frac{1}{1+y^{2}}dx+\frac{1}{1+(\frac{x}{1+y^{2}})^{2}}\frac{x(-2y)}{(1+y^{2})^{2}}dy\\ &=\frac{1+y^{2}}{(1+y^{2})^{2}+x^{2}}dx-\frac{2xy}{(1+y^{2})^{2}+x^{2}}dy \end{align*}$$

故$dz|_{(1,1)}=\frac{2}{5}dx-\frac{2}{5}dy$

2. $u=\frac{z}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}}$, $du|_{(3,4,5)}$

$$\begin{align*} du &= \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}dy + \frac{\partial u}{\partial z}dz \\ &=z\frac{-x}{(x^{2}+y^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}}dx+z\frac{-y}{(x^{2}+y^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}}dxdy+\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}}dz \end{align*}$$

故$du|_{(3,4,5)}=-\frac{3}{25}dx-\frac{4}{25}dy+\frac{1}{5}dz$
