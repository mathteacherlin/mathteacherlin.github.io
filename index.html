4.5随堂测试

2017-04-10

一、 验证$\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{(5n-4)(5n+1)}$收敛, 并求和

$$\begin{align*} a_{n}&=\frac{1}{(5n-4)(5n+1)}\\ &=\frac{1}{5}(\frac{1}{5n-4}-\frac{1}{5n+1}) \end{align*}$$

故
$$\begin{align*} S_{n}&=a_{1}+a_{2}+\cdots+a_{n}\\ &=\frac{1}{5}[(1-\frac{1}{6})+(\frac{1}{6}-\frac{1}{10})+\cdots+(\frac{1}{5n-4}-\frac{1}{5n+1})]\\ &=\frac{1}{5}(1-\frac{1}{5n+1}) \end{align*}$$
则
$$\begin{align*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}S_{n}=\frac{1}{5} \end{align*}$$
即$\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{(5n-4)(5n+1)}$收敛, 且和为$\frac{1}{5}$.

判断下列级数的敛散性

注意：下列级数均为正项级数

二、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}2^{n}sin\frac{\pi}{3^{n}}$

$$\begin{align*} 2^{n}sin\frac{\pi}{3^{n}} &\leq 2^{n}\frac{\pi}{3^{n}}\\ &= \pi (\frac{2}{3})^{n} \end{align*}$$

而级数$\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty} \pi (\frac{2}{3})^{n}$收敛

故级数$\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}2^{n}sin\frac{\pi}{3^{n}}$收敛.

三、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n\cdot \sqrt[3]{n+1}}$

$$\begin{align*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{\frac{1}{n\cdot \sqrt[3]{n+1}}}{\frac{1}{n\cdot \sqrt[3]{n}}}&=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\sqrt[3]{\frac{n}{n+1}}\\ &=1 \end{align*}$$

而级数
$$\begin{align*} \Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n\cdot \sqrt[3]{n}}&=\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n^{\frac{4}{3}}} \end{align*}$$
收敛

故级数$\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n\cdot \sqrt[3]{n+1}}$收敛.

四、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1\cdot 3\cdot 5 \cdot 7 \cdots (2n-1)}{n!}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{u_{n+1}}{u_{n}}&=\frac{1\cdot 3\cdot 5 \cdot 7 \cdots (2(n+1)-1)}{(n+1)!}\cdot \frac{n!}{1\cdot 3\cdot 5 \cdot 7 \cdots (2n-1)}\\ &=\frac{2n+1}{n+1} \end{align*}$$

则
$$\begin{align*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{u_{n+1}}{u_{n}}&=2>1 \end{align*}$$

故原级数发散.

五、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}(1-cos\frac{\pi}{n})$

$$\begin{align*} 1-cos\frac{\pi}{n} \sim 2(\frac{\pi}{n})^{2}, (n \rightarrow \infty) \end{align*}$$

即
$$\begin{align*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{1-cos\frac{\pi}{n}}{2(\frac{\pi}{n})^{2}}=1 \end{align*}$$
而
$$\begin{align*} \Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}2(\frac{\pi}{n})^{2}&=\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}2\pi^{2}\frac{1}{n^{2}} \end{align*}$$
收敛

故级数$\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}(1-cos\frac{\pi}{n})$收敛.

六、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{2^{n}-n}$

解法一

$$\begin{align*} \frac{u_{n+1}}{u_{n}}&=\frac{1}{2^{n+1}-(n+1)} \cdot \frac{2^{n}-n}{1}\\ &=\frac{2^{n}-n}{2^{n+1}-(n+1)} \end{align*}$$

则
$$\begin{align*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{2^{n}-n}{2^{n+1}-(n+1)}&=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{1-\frac{n}{2^{n}}}{2-\frac{n+1}{2^{n}}}\\ &=\frac{1}{2}\\ &<1 \end{align*}$$
故原级数收敛.

解法二

注意当$n\geq 2$时, $2^{n-1}\geq n$.
故当$n\geq 2$时,
$$\begin{align*} 2^{n}-n &\geq 2^{n}-2^{n-1}\\ &=2\cdot 2^{n-1}-2^{n-1}\\ &=2^{n-1} \end{align*}$$
即
$$\begin{align*} \frac{1}{2^{n}-n}\leq \frac{1}{2^{n-1}}, n\geq 2 \end{align*}$$
而级数$\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{2^{n-1}}$收敛

故级数$\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{2^{n}-n}$收敛.

七、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}(\frac{3n+7}{4n-3})^{\frac{n}{2}}$

$$\begin{align*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\sqrt[n]{(\frac{3n+7}{4n-3})^{\frac{n}{2}}}&=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}(\frac{3n+7}{4n-3})^{\frac{1}{2}}\\ &=(\frac{3}{4})^{\frac{1}{2}}\\ &=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\\ &<1 \end{align*}$$

故原级数收敛.

八、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n^{n-1}}$

$$\begin{align*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\sqrt[n]{\frac{1}{n^{n-1}}}&=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\sqrt[n]{\frac{n}{n^{n}}}\\ &=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{\sqrt[n]{n}}{n}\\ &=0\\ &<1 \end{align*}$$

故原级数收敛.

九、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{(lnn)^{n}}$

$$\begin{align*} \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\sqrt[n]{(lnn)^{n}}&=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{1}{lnn}\\ &=0\\ &<1 \end{align*}$$

故原级数收敛.

十、 $\Sigma_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{\sqrt[n]{5}}$

因为$\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{1}{\sqrt[n]{5}}=1 \neq 0$, 故原级数发散.

1. 设$z=u^{2}lnv$, $u=\frac{x}{y}$,$v=3x-2y$,求$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=2ulnv \cdot \frac{1}{y}+u^{2}\frac{1}{v}\cdot 3\\ &=\frac{2xln(3x-2y)}{y^{2}}+\frac{3x^{2}}{(3x-2y)y^{2}}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=2ulnv \cdot \frac{-x}{y^{2}}+u^{2}\frac{1}{v}\cdot (-2)\\ &=-\frac{2x^{2}ln(3x-2y)}{y^{3}}-\frac{2x^{2}}{(3x-2y)y^{2}} \end{align*}$$

2. 设$z=e^{x-2y}$,$y=sinx$,求$\frac{dz}{dx}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{dz}{dx}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\frac{dy}{dx}\\ &=e^{x-2y}+e^{x-2y}(-2)cosx\\ &=(1-2cosx)e^{x-2y} \end{align*}$$

3.求下列符合函数的一阶偏导数, 其中$f$具有一阶连续偏导数:

(1) $z=f(x+y,x-y)$
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=f_{u}^{'}-f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

(2) $z=f(x^{2}-y^{2},e^{xy})$
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=2x f_{u}^{'}+ye^{xy}f_{v}^{'}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=-2yf_{u}^{'}+xe^{xy}f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

(3) $z=f(xcosy,xsiny)$
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=cosy f_{u}^{'}+siny f_{v}^{'}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=-xsiny f_{u}^{'}+xcosy f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

(4) $z=f(x+y,\frac{y}{x})$
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=f_{u}^{'}-\frac{y}{x^{2}} f_{v}^{'}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=f_{u}^{'}+\frac{1}{x} f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

4. 设$y=f(x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2})$, 其中$f$具有一阶连续偏导数, 求$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}$,$\frac{\partial u}{\partial z}$

令$t=x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} &= \frac{du}{dt}\frac{\partial t}{\partial x}\\ &=2x\cdot f^{'} \end{align*}$$
由对称性可知,
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} &=2y\cdot f^{'}\\ \frac{\partial u}{\partial z} &=2z\cdot f^{'}\\ \end{align*}$$

5. 设函数$y=f(x)$是由方程$xy+x+y=1$确定, 求$\frac{dy}{dx}$

令$F(x,y)=xy+x+y-1$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{dy}{dx}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y)}{F_{y}^{'}(x,y)}\\ &=-\frac{y+1}{x+1} \end{align*}$$

6. 设函数$y=f(x)$是由方程$y=1+y^{x}$确定, 求$\frac{dy}{dx}$

令$F(x,y)=1+y^{x}-y$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{dy}{dx}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y)}{F_{y}^{'}(x,y)}\\ &=-\frac{y^{x}lny}{xy^{x-1}-1} \end{align*}$$

7. 设函数$y=f(x)$是由方程$ln\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}=arctan\frac{y}{x}$确定, 求$\frac{dy}{dx}$

令$F(x,y)=ln\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}-arctan\frac{y}{x}$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{dy}{dx}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y)}{F_{y}^{'}(x,y)}\\ &=-\frac{ \frac{x}{x^{2}+y^{2}} -\frac{-\frac{y}{x^{2}} } {1+(\frac{y}{x})^{2}} } { \frac{y}{x^{2}+y^{2}} -\frac{\frac{1}{x } } {1+(\frac{y}{x})^{2}} }\\ &=\frac{x+y}{x-y} \end{align*}$$

8. 设二元函数$z=f(x,y)$是由方程$x^{3}+y^{3}+z^{3}-3axyz=0$确定, 求$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$

令$F(x,y)=x^{3}+y^{3}+z^{3}-3axyz$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y,z)}{F_{z}^{'}(x,y,z)}\\ &=-\frac{3x^{2}-3ayz}{3z^{2}-3axy}\\ &=-\frac{x^{2}-ayz}{z^{2}-axy}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=-\frac{F_{y}^{'}(x,y,z)}{F_{z}^{'}(x,y,z)}\\ &=-\frac{3y^{2}-3axz}{3z^{2}-3axy}\\ &=-\frac{y^{2}-axz}{z^{2}-axy} \end{align*}$$

9. 设二元函数$z=f(x,y)$是由方程$x+2y+z-2\sqrt{xyz}=0$确定, 求$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$

令$F(x,y)=x+2y+z-2\sqrt{xyz}$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y,z)}{F_{z}^{'}(x,y,z)}\\ &=-\frac{1-\frac{\sqrt{yz}}{\sqrt{x}}}{ 1-\frac{\sqrt{xy}}{\sqrt{z}} }\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=-\frac{F_{y}^{'}(x,y,z)}{F_{z}^{'}(x,y,z)}\\ &=-\frac{2-\frac{\sqrt{xz}}{\sqrt{y}}}{ 1-\frac{\sqrt{xy}}{\sqrt{z}} } \end{align*}$$

1. 设$z=f(x,v)$,$v=xy$, 其中$f$具有二阶连续偏导数, 求$\frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x^{2}}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} &= f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}y\\ \frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x^{2}} &= \frac{\partial }{\partial x}(\frac{\partial z}{\partial x})\\ &=\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}y)\\ &=\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial x}\\ &=(\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}) + y(\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x})\\ &=(f_{uu}^{''}+f_{uv}^{''}y)+(f_{vu}^{''}+f_{vv}^{''}y)y\\ &=f_{uu}^{''}+2yf_{uv}^{''}+y^{2}f_{vv}^{''} \end{align*}$$

2. 设$z=f(x+y,xy)$,$v=xy$, 其中$f$具有二阶连续偏导数, 求$\frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x^{2}}$,$\frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} &= f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}y\\ \frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x^{2}} &=\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(\frac{\partial z}{\partial x})\\ &=\frac{\partial }{\partial x}(f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}y)\\ &=\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial x}\\ &=(\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}) + y(\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x})\\ &= (f_{uu}^{''}+f_{uv}^{''}y)+y(f_{vu}^{''}+f_{vv}^{''}y)\\ &=f_{uu}^{''}+2yf_{uv}^{''}+y^{2}f_{vv}^{''}\\ \frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y} &=\frac{\partial }{\partial y}(\frac{\partial z}{\partial x})\\ &=\frac{\partial }{\partial y}(f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}y)\\ &=\frac{\partial }{\partial y}f_{u}^{'}+\frac{\partial }{\partial y}(f_{v}^{'}y)\\ &=\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial y}+(y\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial y}+f_{v}^{'})\\ &=(\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial f_{u}^{'}}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}) + y(\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial f_{v}^{'}}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y})+f_{v}^{'}\\ &= (f_{uu}^{''}+f_{uv}^{''}x)+y(f_{vu}^{''}+f_{vv}^{''}x)+f_{v}^{'}\\ &=f_{uu}^{''}+(x+y)f_{uv}^{''}+xyf_{vv}^{''}+f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

一、

1. 求下列各函数的全微分：

(1) $z=x^{3}+y^{3}-3xy$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=(3x^{2}-3y)dx+(3y^{2}-3x)dy \end{align*}$$

(2) $z=x^{2}y^{3}$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=2xy^{3}dx+3x^{2}y^{2}dy \end{align*}$$

(3) $z=\frac{x^2-y^{2}}{x^{2}+y^{2}}$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=\frac{4xy^{2}}{(x^{2}+y^{2})^{2}}dx-\frac{4x^{2}y}{(x^{2}+y^{2})^{2}}dy \end{align*}$$
注意：$z=\frac{x^{2}-y^{2}}{x^{2}+y^{2}}=1-2\frac{y^{2}}{x^{2}+y^2}=-1+2\frac{x^{2}}{x^{2}+y^{2}}$

(4) $z=sin^{2}x+cos^{2}y$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=2sinxcosxdx+2cosy(-siny)dy\\ &=sin2x dx - sin2y dy \end{align*}$$

(5) $z=yx^{y}$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=yyx^{y-1}dx+(x^{y}+yx^{y}lnx)dy\\ &=y^{2}x^{y-1}dx+(1+ylnx)x^{y}dy \end{align*}$$
注意：对$x$还是对$y$求偏导数

(6) $z=ln(x^{2}+y^{2})$
$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=\frac{2x}{x^{2}+y^{2}}dx+\frac{2y}{x^{2}+y^{2}}dy \end{align*}$$

2. 求函数$z=\frac{y}{x}$在$x=2$,$y=1$,$\Delta x=0.1$,$\Delta y=-0.2$时的全增量$\Delta z$和全微分$dz$

$$\begin{align*} \Delta z &= f(x+\Delta x,y+\Delta y)-f(x,y)\\ &= f(2.1,0.8)-f(2,1)\\ &= \frac{0.8}{2.1}-\frac{1}{2}\\ &= -\frac{5}{42}\\ dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=-\frac{y}{x^{2}}dx+\frac{1}{x}dy \end{align*}$$

故$dz|_{(2,1)}=-\frac{1}{4}dx+\frac{1}{2}dy=-\frac{1}{4}\cdot 0.1+\frac{1}{2}\cdot (-0.2)=-0.125$

3. 求函数$z=ln(2+x^{2}+y^{2})$在$x=2$,$y=1$时的全微分

$$\begin{align*} dz &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy \\ &=\frac{2x}{2+x^{2}+y^{2}}dx+\frac{2y}{2+x^{2}+y^{2}}dy \end{align*}$$

故$dz|_{(2,1)}=\frac{4}{7}dx+\frac{2}{7}dy$

(1) $u=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}$
$$\begin{align*} du &= \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}dy + \frac{\partial u}{\partial z}dz\\ &=\frac{x}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}}dx+\frac{y}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}}dy+\frac{z}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}}dz \end{align*}$$

(2) $u=e^{x+yz}$
$$\begin{align*} du &= \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}dx + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}dy + \frac{\partial u}{\partial z}dz\\ &=e^{x+yz}dx+ze^{x+yz}dy+ye^{x+yz}dz \end{align*}$$
