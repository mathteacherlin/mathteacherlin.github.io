一、

1. 设$z=u^{2}lnv$, $u=\frac{x}{y}$,$v=3x-2y$,求$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=2ulnv \cdot \frac{1}{y}+u^{2}\frac{1}{v}\cdot 3\\ &=\frac{2xln(3x-2y)}{y^{2}}+\frac{3x^{2}}{(3x-2y)y^{2}}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} &= \frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=2ulnv \cdot \frac{-x}{y^{2}}+u^{2}\frac{1}{v}\cdot (-2)\\ &=-\frac{2x^{2}ln(3x-2y)}{y^{3}}-\frac{2x^{2}}{(3x-2y)y^{2}} \end{align*}$$

2. 设$z=e^{x-2y}$,$y=sinx$,求$\frac{dz}{dx}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{dz}{dx}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\frac{dy}{dx}\\ &=e^{x-2y}+e^{x-2y}(-2)cosx\\ &=(1-2cosx)e^{x-2y} \end{align*}$$

3.求下列符合函数的一阶偏导数, 其中$f$具有一阶连续偏导数:

(1) $z=f(x+y,x-y)$
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=f_{u}^{'}-f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

(2) $z=f(x^{2}-y^{2},e^{xy})$
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=2x f_{u}^{'}+ye^{xy}f_{v}^{'}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=-2yf_{u}^{'}+xe^{xy}f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

(3) $z=f(xcosy,xsiny)$
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=cosy f_{u}^{'}+siny f_{v}^{'}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=-xsiny f_{u}^{'}+xcosy f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

(4) $z=f(x+y,\frac{y}{x})$
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\\ &=f_{u}^{'}-\frac{y}{x^{2}} f_{v}^{'}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\\ &=f_{u}^{'}+\frac{1}{x} f_{v}^{'} \end{align*}$$

4. 设$y=f(x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2})$, 其中$f$具有一阶连续偏导数, 求$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}$,$\frac{\partial u}{\partial z}$

令$t=x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} &= \frac{du}{dt}\frac{\partial t}{\partial x}\\ &=2x\cdot f^{'} \end{align*}$$
由对称性可知,
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} &=2y\cdot f^{'}\\ \frac{\partial u}{\partial z} &=2z\cdot f^{'}\\ \end{align*}$$

5. 设函数$y=f(x)$是由方程$xy+x+y=1$确定, 求$\frac{dy}{dx}$

令$F(x,y)=xy+x+y-1$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{dy}{dx}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y)}{F_{y}^{'}(x,y)}\\ &=-\frac{y+1}{x+1} \end{align*}$$

6. 设函数$y=f(x)$是由方程$y=1+y^{x}$确定, 求$\frac{dy}{dx}$

令$F(x,y)=1+y^{x}-y$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{dy}{dx}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y)}{F_{y}^{'}(x,y)}\\ &=-\frac{y^{x}lny}{xy^{x-1}-1} \end{align*}$$

7. 设函数$y=f(x)$是由方程$ln\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}=arctan\frac{y}{x}$确定, 求$\frac{dy}{dx}$

令$F(x,y)=ln\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}-arctan\frac{y}{x}$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{dy}{dx}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y)}{F_{y}^{'}(x,y)}\\ &=-\frac{ \frac{x}{x^{2}+y^{2}} -\frac{-\frac{y}{x^{2}} } {1+(\frac{y}{x})^{2}} } { \frac{y}{x^{2}+y^{2}} -\frac{\frac{1}{x } } {1+(\frac{y}{x})^{2}} }\\ \frac{x+y}{x-y} \end{align*}$$

8. 设二元函数$z=f(x,y)$是由方程$x^{3}+y^{3}+z^{3}-3axyz=0$确定, 求$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$

令$F(x,y)=x^{3}+y^{3}+z^{3}-3axyz$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y,z)}{F_{z}^{'}(x,y,z)}\\ &=-\frac{3x^{2}-3ayz}{3z^{2}-3axy}\\ &=-\frac{x^{2}-ayz}{z^{2}-axy}\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=-\frac{F_{y}^{'}(x,y,z)}{F_{z}^{'}(x,y,z)}\\ &=-\frac{3y^{2}-3axz}{3z^{2}-3axy}\\ &=-\frac{y^{2}-axz}{z^{2}-axy} \end{align*}$$

9. 设二元函数$z=f(x,y)$是由方程$x+2y+z-2\sqrt{xyz}=0$确定, 求$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$

令$F(x,y)=x+2y+z-2\sqrt{xyz}$, 则
$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}&=-\frac{F_{x}^{'}(x,y,z)}{F_{z}^{'}(x,y,z)}\\ &=-\frac{1-\frac{\sqrt{yz}}{\sqrt{x}}}{ 1-\frac{\sqrt{xy}}{\sqrt{z}} }\\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}&=-\frac{F_{y}^{'}(x,y,z)}{F_{z}^{'}(x,y,z)}\\ &=-\frac{2-\frac{\sqrt{xz}}{\sqrt{y}}}{ 1-\frac{\sqrt{xy}}{\sqrt{z}} } \end{align*}$$

二、

1. 设$z=f(x,v)$,$v=xy$, 其中$f$具有二阶连续偏导数, 求$\frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x^{2}}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} &= f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}y\\ \frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x^{2}} &= (f_{uu}^{''}+f_{uv}^{''}y)+(f_{vu}^{''}+f_{vv}^{''}y)y\\ &=f_{uu}^{''}+2yf_{uv}^{''}+y^{2}f_{vv}^{''} \end{align*}$$

2. 设$z=f(x+y,xy)$,$v=xy$, 其中$f$具有二阶连续偏导数, 求$\frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x^{2}}$,$\frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y}$

$$\begin{align*} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} &= f_{u}^{'}+f_{v}^{'}y\\ \frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x^{2}} &= (f_{uu}^{''}+f_{uv}^{''}y)+(f_{vu}^{''}+f_{vv}^{''}y)y\\ &=f_{uu}^{''}+2yf_{uv}^{''}+y^{2}f_{vv}^{''}\\ \frac{\partial^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y} &= (f_{uu}^{''}+f_{uv}^{''}x)+(f_{vu}^{''}+f_{vv}^{''}x)y\\ &=f_{uu}^{''}+(x+y)f_{uv}^{''}+xyf_{vv}^{''}\ \end{align*}$$
